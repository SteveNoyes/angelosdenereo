Her nice car is just a moon,
No need with gas, it runs on troon.
She's not alone she brings a table,
a plate of fish, her cats' own cradle.

The fish likes to chase a glass,
Especially one that's in the middle class.
The artist shudders at the ring
He want to leave, she wants the thing.

Painting represents the place where the conscious and unconscious meet. Exploring the point where who we are and who we want to be grapple with their places in our ever-changing life.

Biography

Paris-based artist born in Genoa. After moving to the UK as a child, went south to study Art and Art History, and received a BFA with Honors

Angelos’ paintings are included in private and public collections in Europe, the UK and the US.
