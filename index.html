Angelos DéNeréo

Of all we left, for good

Whose apple is that? I think I know.
Its owner is quite lost and though,
she was cross like a dark abeto.
I watch her pace. I cry hello.

Tormented with nightmares she never sleeps.
Revenge is a promise a girl should keep.
